Sloppy Joes

Some tasty and fancy sloppy Joes

This is a fancy recipe for elevated sloppy Joes. An American classic. Everyone loves sloppy joes!

This is a recipe you should use when you want some meaty sammiches.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of Ground Chuck (80% lean)
  • Some tomato paste
  • Some brown sugar
  • Catchup (ketchup)
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Worchester Sauce
  • Corn Startch
  • Baking Soda
  • paprika
  • a nice big yellow onion
  • some garlic cloves
  • Pickle rounds
  • hamburger buns

Steps

  1. First throw half onion in a large non-stick skillet, with some baking soda and a bit of vegeble oil
  2. Cook the onions til smelling good, somewhat transparent
  3. Thrown in the garlic
  4. Throw in all them spices
  5. Then all the rest of the stuff (hold the beef)
  6. The finally throw in that beef and start mixin and cookin
  7. Add a whole bunch of spices
  8. Boil the pasta separately and dump that in the pan
  9. Mix it all up
  10. Eat the tasty meal

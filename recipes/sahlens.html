Sahlens Hotdogs

A Buffalo Favorite

This is a simple recipe to cook some hotdogs

If you don't have a grill, but want some hotdogs... do this!

Ingredients

  • A pack of Sahlens hotdogs
  • Hotdog Buns
  • A jar of Vlasik stackers pickels
  • A bottle of yellow mustard

Steps

  1. First, line a cookie sheet with aluminum foil
  2. Put your hotdogs on the sheet
  3. Turn your oven broiler on HIGH
  4. Put hotdogs in the oven
  5. after a few minutes, turn the hotdogs a bit
  6. Add the tomatoes
  7. Add a whole bunch of spices
  8. Broil hotdogs until splitting, bubbling, crispy
  9. Throw buns in oven for a minute or two
  10. Assemble your hotodgs in buns with yellow mustard and stackers pickels
